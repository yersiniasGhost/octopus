Now Accepting Applications for Ohio's Summer Crisis Program
As temperatures rise across Ohio, we want to ensure that no family has to choose between staying cool and paying other essential bills. The Ohio Summer Crisis Program is now accepting applications, and we encourage eligible households to enroll as soon as possible.

What is the Summer Crisis Program?
The Summer Crisis Program provides financial assistance to help eligible Ohio households pay their electric bills during the hot summer months. This vital program helps families maintain air conditioning and other essential cooling services when they need them most.
