# Analysis-03: Email + Text Campaign Results
## One-Page Executive Summary

**Date**: December 8, 2025 | **Participants**: 7,451 | **Campaigns**: 143 (68 email + 74 text)

---

## Key Finding: Text Messaging is 5.3x More Effective

| Channel | Engagement Rate | Impact |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Email Clicks | 2.18% | Baseline |
| Text Replies | **11.54%** | **+430%** |
| Combined | **8.54%** | Multi-channel wins |

---

## Top Performer Segment: 41.1% Engagement

**292 "Super Performers"** engage at nearly 5x the average rate. Their profile:
- Moderate campaign exposure (26 touches)
- Multi-channel (email + text)
- Heavy personalized/motivational messaging
- Middle income ($30K), moderate energy burden (10%)

---

## 3 Actionable Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Expected Impact |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **1** | Increase text campaign frequency | +3-5x engagement lift |
| **2** | Add text to email-only campaigns | Convert 3,170 email-only participants |
| **3** | Target 25-40 campaign sweet spot | Optimal engagement zone |
